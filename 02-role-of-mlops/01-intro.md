# MLOps in Practice: Hello World Project

## Overview

Having covered the theory of MLOps, we now move into a hands-on, practical understanding of how MLOps engineers collaborate with data scientists and ML engineers in real-world scenarios.

---

## Business Requirement

> **Goal:** Build a system to predict **flower species** based on the following inputs:
> - Petal Length
> - Petal Width
> - Sepal Length
> - Sepal Width

---

## What We'll Cover

The upcoming lectures will walk through this project in four stages:

1. **Data Scientist's Role** — What activities do data scientists perform to tackle this business requirement?
2. **MLOps Engineer (Stage 1)** — How do MLOps engineers support data scientists?
3. **ML Engineer's Role** — How do ML engineers take the model to the next level (productionization)?
4. **MLOps Engineer (Stage 2)** — How do MLOps engineers support ML engineers?
